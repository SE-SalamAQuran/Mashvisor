Here is a Set of Solutions For The Regular Real Estate Troubles

1) Less-time consuming

Beginning real estate investors usually spend 3 months whenever they are conducting research about markets and specific rental properties. As a result, they close fewer deals compared to more experienced real estate investors. With Mashvisor, the research process can be completed in just 15 minutes.

2) Faster investment analysis

Real estate investors, sometimes, are not able to analyze and evaluate off-market rental properties fast enough before other investors or home buyers purchase those properties. Mashvisor solves this problem by allowing investors to quickly compare the performance of an off-market rental property to that of other rental properties in the same area in terms of startup and recurring costs, expected rental income, cash flow, cash on cash return, cap rate, and occupancy rate. In addition, the platform provides investors with all the data they need about the owners of off-market properties and permits them to communicate with them directly within Mashvisor.

3) Guaranteed to get qualified leads.

Real estate agents may successfully generate potential buyers of rental properties, but they are not given a way to find out if these leads are qualified and worthy of their time. Sometimes, they are spending a lot of weeks engaging with potential property buyers who end up not purchasing the property. Through the aid of Mashvisor’s lead qualification tool, real estate agents are now capable of knowing whether or not a lead is more likely to buy a property in the coming weeks or months.
